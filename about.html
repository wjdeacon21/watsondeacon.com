Bio

Watson is from Kansas City and lives in New York. Usually, he writes about people he knows and things he sees to try to know and see them more truly. He studied English, philosophy, and religion at Washington & Lee in Virginia, where his poems were selected for the George A. Mahan award in 2023 and the Academy of American Poets award in 2024. His work can be found in ARTHOUSE FREE PRESS, Ampersand, and Spare magazines.
